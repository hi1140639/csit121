French Toast

Recipe by Jan Bittner on All Recipes

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
    •
    1. Gather all ingredients
    2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon and salt together in a bowl
    3. Butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat
    4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides
    5. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook unitl golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side
    6. Serve hot

    This is my favorite recipe because I love the smell of cinnamon especially around the holidays.
